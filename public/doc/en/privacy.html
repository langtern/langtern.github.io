The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to provide information on the rights of users, set out in the General Data Protection Regulation (Reglamento General de Protección de Datos, “RGPD”) of the Spanish legislation.

1.- DATA STORAGE AND PROCESSING

Langtern is a standalone application that operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you create within the app, including flashcard decks, learning progress, and preferences, is stored locally on your device.

Since all data remains on your device: * We have no access to your personal information * We do not create user accounts or profiles on our servers * We do not track your usage or behavior * We do not share any data with third parties for analytics or advertising purposes

You are solely responsible for backing up your data. If you uninstall the app or lose your device, your data may be permanently lost.

You can find more information about what data is stored locally in our mobile applications on Google Play (for Chinese and English) and in the Apple App Store (for Chinese and English).

2.- USER RIGHTS

Since Langtern does not collect or process personal data on external servers, the traditional data protection rights (access, rectification, deletion, portability, etc.) are exercised directly by you through the app itself. You have full control over your data:

  1. Access your data at any time within the app.

  2. Modify or correct any data you have entered.

  3. Delete any or all of your data through the app’s settings or by uninstalling the application.

  4. Export your data using the app’s export functionality, where available.

If you have any questions about your data or this privacy policy, you can contact us at: * Email: contact@langtern.com

3.- SECURITY MEASURES

Your data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device’s security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend that you:

  • Keep your device’s operating system updated
  • Use a secure screen lock
  • Be cautious about granting other apps access to your device’s storage

Langtern does not have access to your data and therefore cannot guarantee its security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

4.- EXEMPTION OF LIABILITY

We will respond only for what happens in relation to this app, and therefore with its Terms and Conditions, and its Privacy Policy.

In the event that the user is redirected to third-party websites or platforms, their legal texts will apply, and Langtern will not be responsible.

Please consult the privacy policy of any website you visit through the web assistant and understand that you may be bound by their terms when browsing them.
